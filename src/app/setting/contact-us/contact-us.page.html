EASTWEAR is an AR IOS app that offers a brand-new digital fashion experience to users,
where users can:

a) Create a real-size digital avatar with digital fashion collections
b) Try on the digital clothes in real-time through an Augmented Reality (AR) camera
c) Watch virtual digital fashion runway

Digital fashion is virtual clothing built using 3D software and computer technologies, which is visualized and stimulated to look like real garments. All digital collections in EasyWear are created in collaborations with Hong Kong fashion designers who want to exhibit their works in a digital way.
